Our Story

At Dessert Co, we're passionate about bringing a slice of sweetness to the heart of Melbourne. Founded in 2024 by pastry enthusiast, Emma Taylor, our story began with a dream to create a haven for dessert lovers in the CBD. Growing up in Melbourne, Emma was inspired by the city's vibrant food culture and decided to pursue her passion for baking after completing a culinary degree at the prestigious Melbourne Institute of Culinary Arts. With years of experience working in top Melbourne restaurants, Emma's vision was to create a unique dessert experience that would tantalize the taste buds of locals and visitors alike.

The Owner

Emma's love and passion with desserts began at a young age, when she would spend hours in the kitchen with her grandmother, learning the art of traditional baking. As she grew older, her fascination with the world of sweets only intensified, and she began experimenting with innovative flavors and techniques. After completing her culinary degree, Emma worked in some of Melbourne's top restaurants, honing her skills and building a reputation as a talented pastry chef.

The Journey

In 2024, Emma decided to take the leap and open her own dessert shop, Dessert Co, in the heart of Melbourne's CBD. Her vision was to create a welcoming space where dessert lovers could come together to indulge in a wide range of sweet treats, from classic cupcakes and macarons to decadent cake slices and artisanal chocolates. With a focus on using only the freshest, locally-sourced ingredients, Emma's creations are designed to delight the senses and leave a lasting impression.

Today, Dessert Co is a beloved destination for anyone seeking a sweet escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. Our cozy store is designed to feel like a warm hug, with comfortable seating areas and vintage-inspired decor that will transport you back to a bygone era. Whether you're a foodie, a sweet-tooth, or simply someone who appreciates the art of fine desserts, we invite you to come and experience the magic of Dessert Co for yourself.
